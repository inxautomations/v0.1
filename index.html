Frequently Asked Questions

Ques:- Are there any scholarship programs?

Ans:- Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships ranging from 10% to 50% fee waiver for deserving students based on entrance test performance.

Ques:- Do you provide study material and test series?

Ans:- Yes, comprehensive study material and regular test series are provided for all courses to ensure complete exam-oriented preparation.

Ques:- Are online or hybrid classes available?

Ans:- Yes, we offer both offline classroom programs and selected online or hybrid options depending on the course and batch availability.

Ques:- How are batches structured and what is the batch size?

Ans:- Batches are structured based on student level and exam goals, with limited batch sizes to ensure personal attention and effective learning.

Ques:- Is doubt-clearing support available outside class hours?

Ans:- Yes, dedicated doubt-clearing sessions and one-on-one support are available to help students fully understand concepts and improve performance.
